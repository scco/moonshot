What

We've now got programmable money, and for the first time ever it's possible to program our values into our money. We value coordination, so why not use programmable money to create better coordination tools? The moonshot collective is a collection of builders & web3 community members who are looking to prototype experiments in coordination (whether thats public goods, private goods, governance tools).
